Peking Opera, as the treasure of traditional Chinese opera art, was once the exclusive entertainment of the rich and aristocrats during the Qing Dynasty and the Republic of China. Due to the restrictions of economic conditions and the estrangement of social classes, it is difficult for ordinary people to have access to this art form. However, with the development of The Times and the progress of science and technology, the way of transmission and viewing of Peking Opera have undergone earth-shaking changes. Today, Peking Opera has become an entertainment that the general public can access and enjoy.

Since modern times, Peking Opera has gradually gone abroad and entered the stage around the world, becoming an important bridge of cultural exchange between China and foreign countries. During this period, the spread of Peking Opera mainly depended on the running of opera troupes. But in modern times, with the use of digital technology, Beijing Opera has made new breakthroughs in both performance forms and dissemination. Beijing Opera has shown a highly digital development trend in China.
